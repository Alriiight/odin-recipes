Mango Avocado Salad

Rainbow of Flavor

Have you ever wanted, or better yet, craved with all your heart something fresh and delicious during those hot summer days? Today we bring you a recipe that will entice all your taste buds and keep you fresh and full for hours.

This recipe will make you feel like you're enjoying a feast in the Caribbean, with fresh cut avocadoes and mangos, we can't think of a better combination. This easy to make recipe can be an everyday no brainer, trust us. Give it a try and let us know how much you love it.

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • Avocados
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Red Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Dice the onions and mince the garlic
  2. Peel the avocados and mangoes and cube them into small pieces. Cut tomatoes in halves.
  3. Place all the ingredients in a bowl and squeeze in the juice of one lemon.
  4. Add salt and olive oil to taste.
  5. Toss to mix together well.
  6. Enjoy by itself or serve as a side dish.
